Saving To Draft

For saving an article the user must go to YOUR ARTICLES->DRAFT

Helps the user to save their content in their Draft. So that they resume their content whenever it is required. And when the Article is completed and is ready to publish, so when the user is submitted the article is not published unless it is proof read by Moderator. So whenever the user posts an article it comes to the access of Moderator through which it is published in the web. .

Through which you the article is saved.
